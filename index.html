Grades are posted on Blackboard after each lab is graded. Verify your posted grade matches your returned report. For missing or disputed grades, email your lab section TAs with your name, section, and the experiment number.

The lab weight toward the final course grade is determined by the individual lecture instructor.

FAQ

Where do I find my lab schedule?

Course-specific schedules are linked under General Info above. You can also download the full A/B Cycle Calendar PDF.

What should I bring to lab?

Bring a printed copy of the lab manual for your experiment. TAs will not have extra copies. The prelab (first page) is collected at the start of the session.

How do I check or dispute my lab grade?

Grades are posted on Blackboard after each lab is graded. If a grade is missing or you believe it is incorrect, email your lab section TAs with your name, section, and the experiment number.

Are there labs during Spring Break?

No. Labs are suspended during Spring Break and on designated study break days, as shown in the calendar above.

How much does lab count toward my final grade?

The weighting varies by course and instructor. Consult your course syllabus or ask your lecture instructor.
